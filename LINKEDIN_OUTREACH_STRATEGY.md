## ðŸš¨ **Red Flags & Solutions**

### **Problem:** Low connection acceptance
**Solution:** Personalize every note, mention specific work, offer immediate value

### **Problem:** No response to messages
**Solution:** Test different value propositions, use social proof, reduce ask size

### **Problem:** High interest but no action
**Solution:** Create lower-commitment entry points, offer pilot programs, provide ROI calculators

### **Problem:** Content not resonating
**Solution:** A/B test formats, survey audience, analyze competitors, focus on pain points
